Old boy

This movie has done irrepairable damage to me and I wished I never watched this film. Now although this film has left a bad taste in my mouth I have to admit this is an excellent film for its execution. This film has one of the most impressive one-take fighting scene and it is very entertainingly quirky but its plot twist is the highlight of this movie. I know I shouldn't be mad at this since that was the whole point but I'm was a 15 year old at the time so I was disgusted and it gave me a feeling I've never felt before. Love it or hate it, but there is one thing all of us who have seen this film will agree upon and that is we will never forget this film
