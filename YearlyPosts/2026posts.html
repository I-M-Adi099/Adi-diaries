My Dad bought a shitty phone

Date: 05-02-26

My Dad bought a cheap iphone knockoff for only 7,200 rupees. Even though the phone is for my mom and she doesn't need a powehouse of a phone, I still can't help but cringe at the fact that its a blatant rip-off and overall a shit phone.

        Here is the specs:
        RAM and ROM:    6GB and 128GB
        Front camera:   8MP
        Back camera:    20MP
        Battery:        5000mAh
        CPU:            (I don't even know, but its shitty and it gets hot easily)

The only good thing I can think is that it came with a free headphone, it's 5G and it has a micro sd slot. The speaker is worse than my father's current phone, you can't even video call with it and most of the pre-installed apps (except google-apps) are all ancient apps that I remember from back when I was in class 3.

I joined a quiz competition

Date: 24-01-26

I still can't believe it... but I won the competition!

So you see there was a quiz competition happening at Navodaya vidyalaya Kakching, Manipur and I was asked to joined the competition. Even though I wanted to decline it so bad, the teachers were looking at me with such anticipation and expectation that I failed to do so properly (I said I did not want to do it).

So I looked at the bright side and thought "I am going to have a new experience!". The teacher said it was going to be a team competition So, I decided to shift my responsibility to others which I am not proud of but she was kind and smart. I want to be somewhat more responsible next time.

Ultimately, the day of the competition came and I thought I was the most unprepared out of the three students from our school but the competition turned out to be a MCQ and So, I gambled and... I somehow won the first prize! LIKE BRO WHAT!? I admit I knew about 60% of the questions but some where pure guess (Also the quiz was a inter-school competition). I didn't know how to feel about this I felt very undeserving of this and also I thought of huge expectation the teachers would place on me now.

But I won I guess and the prize was 1500 rupees. Also the quiz was just a part of a program to raise awareness of operation sindoor.

Practical exam

Date: 10-01-26

So, we finally did the practical exam... and it was a lot more fun than I thought it would be.

You see because we did the practical classes so late, we had to cram all the necessary ones in a short amount of time and it was a lot of fun even though I think I am somewhat bad at it.

But during the course of the practical classes only three students were regular for boys (for girls it was 7-8). And becausse of that many student's were confused on what to write on the practical notebook. Some of my classmates submitted the practical notebooks during the exams.

As for the practical exam itself... it was a complete joke. We didn't even do any practical tasks (for our school), we just copied the answers from the practical notebook and that's it.
